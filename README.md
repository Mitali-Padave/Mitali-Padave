## 🌱 About Me

I'm an aspiring **Data Analyst** who genuinely enjoys the moment when a messy spreadsheet finally tells a clear story.

I'm early in my journey, and I learn best by **building real things**. I take a raw dataset, clean it, ask "so what does this actually mean for a business?", and turn the answer into a dashboard someone could decide from.

- 🔭 Currently building an **Excel data analytics portfolio** (5 projects and counting)
- 🌍 Based in Maharashtra, India
- 💡 I care about getting the analysis *right*. I'd rather validate a finding than ship a pretty chart that isn't true.
- 🎯 Goal: grow into a data analyst who solves real business problems, not just makes reports
